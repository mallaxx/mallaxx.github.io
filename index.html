The Problem: Food Waste at Home
  • 47% of food waste globally comes from households.

  • Wasting food means wasting the resources used to grow, transport, and store it.

  • Wasted food costs the average household $1,500 annually.
Why Does Food Go to Waste at Home?
"Common Causes of Household Food Waste"
  • Forgotten Pantry Items: People often buy items they already have.

  • Expired Food: Food left unused for too long in the fridge or pantry.

  • Lack of Meal Planning: Households fail to plan meals around the ingredients they already own.
Our Solution: Frigilog
Frigilog is an innovative food management app designed to minimize food waste and promote efficient pantry usage by offering the following features:
  • Seamless Pantry Tracking: Effortlessly scan or manually enter items to keep a real-time inventory and track expiration dates.
  • Personalized Recipe Suggestions: Receive tailored recipe recommendations based on the ingredients currently available in your pantry.
  • Smart Meal Planning: Create customized meal plans that utilize your pantry items, ensuring nothing goes to waste.
  • Timely Expiration Notifications: Get alerts for items approaching their expiration dates to help you use them before they go bad.
How Frigilog Works
The user journey with Frigilog:
  • Step 1: Users scan grocery items to add them to their pantry inventory.
  • Step 2: The app tracks expiration dates and sends alerts for items nearing expiration.
  • Step 3: Frigilog recommends recipes based on available pantry ingredients.
  • Step 4: Users can plan meals and shop efficiently to minimize food waste.
Smart Recipe Recommendations
Frigilog offers tailored recipe suggestions to help reduce waste:
  • Dynamic Recipe Suggestions: Users receive recipes based on ingredients currently in their pantry.
  • Dietary Preferences: Users can filter recipes by dietary restrictions such as vegan, gluten-free, etc.
  • Seasonal and Trending Recipes: Highlight popular recipes that utilize available pantry items.
